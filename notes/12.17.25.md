**ChatGPT Images 1.5 Release**
	- OpenAI released a new ChatGPT Images powered by the GPT Image 1.5 model 
	- ChatGPT Images generates and edits images up to four times faster than prior versions
	- Allows more accurate generation and editing 
	- There is a new section in ChatGPT to allow preset styles, filters, and creative prompts for users to access image generation easier
	- The model improves photo edits, style transformations, and visuals to images
