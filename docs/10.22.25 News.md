**AutoLearn**
	- New program that monitors your AI agent's processing steps and essentially creates "skills" depending on the reasoning steps the AI agent took
	- This allows for future requests to be completed with previous correct reasoning than having to rethink and re-reason the processing steps for the prompt
	- Could be extremely useful because it allows AI agents to be specialized/trained greatly and in depth towards specific tasks
